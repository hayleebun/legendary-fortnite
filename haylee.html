Karaoke

I feel like the biggest misconception about karaoke is that you have to be a good singer to enjoy it. The secret is that you don't have to be good, you just have to be a good performer and it's fun. That said, there are certain songs that are a must when I go and certain songs I think should be banned from karaoke rooms and karaoke stages alike.

Music, Games, and Concerts

I've always loved music! When I was younger, I was convinced that I would study music therapy at university (before I learned how much schooling it requires and how little they make).
Nowadays, I just use music as a way to relax and have fun. My favorite kind of games are music and rhythm games. I also go to concerts with friends. It's a good reason to dress up in clothes I wouldn't wear anywhere else, so I usually enjoy myself and take lots of pictures.
